About the CIS Club

Our Mission

The CIS Club aims to connect students through technology, collaboration, and innovation. We provide opportunities for learning, networking, and skill development through events, workshops, and community-driven projects.

Our History

Founded in 2015, the CIS Club has grown into a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, we have hosted hackathons, guest lectures, coding competitions, and hands-on workshops to empower students and prepare them for careers in IT and programming.
